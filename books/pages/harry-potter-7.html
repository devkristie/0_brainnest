Harry Potter And The Deathly Hallows

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows is a fantasy novel written by British author J. K. Rowling.

It was released on 21 July 2007 in the United Kingdom by Bloomsbury and is the seventh and final novel of the main Harry Potter series.

What the book is about

The novel chronicles the events directly following Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (2005) and the final confrontation between the wizards Harry Potter and Lord Voldemort.
